Syntax Note: Summing Processes
•Processes with a guarded choice construct are called normal processes •Such processes may be summed together, to create a third process, with a combined choice construct, e.g.
A::= x ? [] , A1 ;
       y ? [] , A2 .
B::= z ? [] , B1 ;
       w ? [] , B2 .
C::= A + B .
x ? [] , A1 ;
y ? [] , A2 ;
z ? [] , B1 ;
 w ? [] , B2 .
