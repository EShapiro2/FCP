Monte-Carlo Algorithm for Exact Stochastic Simulation of Coupled Chemical Reactions
•A Monte-Carlo technique to simulate the stochastic process described by P(t,m)
1.Initialization:  Set t0, tstop, reaction parameters c1-cM and types, species quantities X1-XN.
2.Monte-Carlo step: Select random t and m according to P(t,m) (based on all hici)
3.Update system: Advance t and update products and reactant species to reflect one Rm reaction. Re-calculate hici for reactions whose reactants have changed
4.Repeat 2-3 until t >= tstop
