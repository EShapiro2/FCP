Coupled chemical reactions as
stochastic processes*
1. Xi - current number of molecules of chemical
species Si in V.
2. Rm (m=1,…,M) – The (elementary) chemical
reactions in which the N species may participate
3. cm, reaction parameters. cmdt  is the average
probability that a particular combination of Rm
reactant molecules will react accordingly in the
next time interval dt.
*Full details in Gillespie 76,77
