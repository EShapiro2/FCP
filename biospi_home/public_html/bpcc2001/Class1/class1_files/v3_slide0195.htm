Coupled chemical reactions as
stochastic processes (Gillespie 76,77)*
• N chemical species, each in quantity Xi in volume V
• Can participate in R reactions, each characterized
by a reaction parameter cm (similar to rate
constant)
• What is the next time step and which reaction
would occur in it ?
• hicidt  - probability that the next reaction is Ri,
where hi – number of combinations of reactants
• P(t,m) – probability that the time step is t and the
reaction is Rm
