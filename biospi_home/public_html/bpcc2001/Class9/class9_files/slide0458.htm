Molecular Interactions
•Protein-protein interactions
–Binding or unbinding (formation or breaking of complex)
–Covalent modification:
– phosphorylation (tyr, thr, ser)
–Conformation changes
–Translocation
–Targeting for degradation
•Small molecule regulated events
–Binding or unbinding, resulting in conformation change: Steroid ligand, nucleotide binding
–Production of second messengers (e.g. Ca+2)
