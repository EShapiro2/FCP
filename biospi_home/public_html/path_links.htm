PATHWAYS ON THE WEB

 

A growing number of bioinformatics resources and projects are dedicated to biochemical pathways and processes.

Several formal representation languages have been suggested, including the pi-calculus, and various databases of signal transduction and metabolic pathways are being developed. Collaborative consortiums coordinate some of these efforts.
