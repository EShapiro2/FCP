The lac Operon “logic”
Lactose binds to
the lac repressor
and removes it
from the promoter
Glucose decreases
the concentration
of cAMP, which
releases CAP,
causing it to unbind
from the promoter.
Only when there is
lactose and there
is no glucose will
the CAP be bound
and the repressor
not, and the operon
will be ON
