Covalent and non-covalent association
of phosphate groups
• The association (or absence) of a
phosphate group with a protein
may affect its capability to
interact or its activity
– Activate an enzymatic domain by
conformation change
– Enable or disable binding by
structural change in binding site
– Affect binding/unbinding of
complex and release of “active
form” of a G-protein
• Both the covalent and non-
covalent modifications are
reversible, and so are their
effects.
