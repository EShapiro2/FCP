Adapter proteins:
Coupling receptor and Ras activation
A series of protein-protein binding events follow, leading to the formation of a multi-protein complex at the receptor: First, the SHC adapter protein binds the  receptor through an SH2 domain. The receptor can then phosphorylate it on a Tyr residues, allowing it to bind the SH2 domain of the GRB2 protein, which in parallel can bind the SH3 domain of the SOS protein
