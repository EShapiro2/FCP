Coupled chemical reactions as
stochastic processes*
P (t,m) dt – The probability at time t that the next
reaction in V will occur in the
differential time interval (t+t,t+t+dt)
and will be an Rm reaction
hmcm dt     –    The probability that an Rm reaction will
occur in V in the next time interval dt .
hm             -    Number of distinct molecular reactant
combinations for reaction Rm found in V
at time t. e.g. for the bimolecular
reaction above hm= Xi+Xj
